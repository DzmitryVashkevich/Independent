Lido di Jesolo

Lido di Jesolo in Italy is a modern, but at the same time cozy resort, located in the northeastern part of the Venetian lagoon. Its spacious, well-equipped beaches and quality hotels, often with Russian-speaking staff, guarantee an impeccable beach holiday for the whole family. Hiking in amusement parks, water sports, golf, shopping, visiting restaurants and pubs, as well as exciting excursions to Venice, Verona, Murano and other sights of Italy will help diversify it.
